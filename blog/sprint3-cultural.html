Cultural Blog - Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Two main aspects of EQ are the ability to recognise and understand our own emotions as well as the ability to recognise and understand the emotions of others.

Having emotional intelligence can help manage and regulate one's own emotions and harness these emotions for effective use. It also is useful in recognising and helping the emotions of others.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ is more to do with logic, reasoning and analyzing focusing on problem solving. It is the more traditional way we have measured intelligence in the past.
